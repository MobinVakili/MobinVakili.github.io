About me

Welcome to my website! I am an Environmental Engineer with three years of research experience in the optimization of environmental systems using artificial intelligence algorithms.

I am proficient in building and operating pilot-scale continuous flow systems, and skilled in the development of prediction and optimization models using Python, R and MATLAB.

I earned my M.Sc. in Environmental Engineering from Amirkabir University of Technology (Tehran Polytechnic) and my B.Sc. in Civil Engineering from Isfahan University of Technology.
